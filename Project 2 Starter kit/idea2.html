IDEA 2

Snapchat dialogue / questions



The found poem is an question and answer game using cards and was made for children. Therefore to reflect the concept of asking questions and getting answers in return, Idea 2 proposes that the questions are asked on Snapchat, and then people are able to reply their own answers. After a while, the real answer will be posted and people will be able to view what the answer was. This will introduce a new level of interactivity to the poem uses snapchat as a platform to play games and ask questions to a specific group of people (who follow you).
